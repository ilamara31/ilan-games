📖 HOW TO PLAY

⏱️ Timing is everything
As the ball arrives, tap / press SPACE to play your shot. Time it in the green middle for a SIX or FOUR 🔥. A bit off = singles; way off = OUT.
🎯 Aim your shot
Swipe or press ← leg side, ↑ straight, → off side just before you hit.
